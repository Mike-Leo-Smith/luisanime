Chapter 1: The Arrival

It was a dark and stormy night. Sarah, a young governess, arrived at the old mansion on the hill to care for two orphaned children, Emily and James. The mansion was isolated, surrounded by overgrown gardens and tall gates.

Mr. Blackwood, the mysterious owner, greeted her at the door. He was a tall man with piercing blue eyes that seemed to look through her. "Welcome to Blackwood Manor," he said. "The children have been expecting you."

Dr. Chen, the elderly family physician, warned Sarah privately: "Be careful here. Strange things happen at night."

That evening, as Sarah tucked the children into bed, Emily whispered: "There's a ghost in the attic, Miss Sarah. We hear it walking at night."
