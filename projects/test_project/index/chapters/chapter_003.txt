Chapter 3: The Secret

That night, Sarah couldn't sleep. She heard footsteps from above - the attic. Gathering her courage, she climbed the stairs. The attic door was unlocked.

Inside, she found Eliza's diary. The final entry revealed the truth: Eliza had discovered a secret about the mansion's past and had been silenced. But she had hidden the evidence in the woods.

Sarah knew she had to find the truth - before she became the next victim.
