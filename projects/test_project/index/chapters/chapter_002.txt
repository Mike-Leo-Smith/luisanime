Chapter 2: The Discovery

The next morning, Sarah explored the grounds. In an old greenhouse at the far end of the property, she found a small golden locket with a strange symbol. When she showed it to Mr. Blackwood, he turned pale.

"Where did you find this?" he demanded.

"In the greenhouse," Sarah replied.

"This belonged to my sister Eliza," he said quietly. "She disappeared ten years ago. We never found her body."
