The Haunted Mansion

Chapter 1: The Arrival

It was a dark and stormy night. Sarah, a young governess, arrived at the old mansion on the hill to care for two orphaned children, Emily and James. The mansion was isolated, surrounded by overgrown gardens and tall gates.

Mr. Blackwood, the mysterious owner, greeted her at the door. He was a tall man with piercing blue eyes that seemed to look through her. "Welcome to Blackwood Manor," he said. "The children have been expecting you."

Dr. Chen, the elderly family physician, warned Sarah privately: "Be careful here. Strange things happen at night."

That evening, as Sarah tucked the children into bed, Emily whispered: "There's a ghost in the attic, Miss Sarah. We hear it walking at night."

Chapter 2: The Discovery

The next morning, Sarah explored the grounds. In an old greenhouse at the far end of the property, she found a small golden locket with a strange symbol. When she showed it to Mr. Blackwood, he turned pale.

"Where did you find this?" he demanded.

"In the greenhouse," Sarah replied.

"This belonged to my sister Eliza," he said quietly. "She disappeared ten years ago. We never found her body."

Chapter 3: The Secret

That night, Sarah couldn't sleep. She heard footsteps from above - the attic. Gathering her courage, she climbed the stairs. The attic door was unlocked.

Inside, she found Eliza's diary. The final entry revealed the truth: Eliza had discovered a secret about the mansion's past and had been silenced. But she had hidden the evidence in the woods.

Sarah knew she had to find the truth - before she became the next victim.
