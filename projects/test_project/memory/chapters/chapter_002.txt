Chapter 2: The Discovery

The next morning, the storm had passed. Sarah woke early and decided to explore the grounds. She walked through the overgrown garden to an old greenhouse at the far end of the property.

Inside, she found signs of recent activity. Fresh footprints in the dirt led to a locked door at the back. Someone had been here recently.

As she turned to leave, she noticed something glinting in the dirt. It was a small golden locket with a strange symbol engraved on it. She pocketed it, deciding to ask Mr. Blackwood about it later.

Back at the house, she found the children, Emily and James, having breakfast in the nursery.

"Miss Sarah!" Emily called out. "James says there's a ghost in the attic!"

"There's no such thing as ghosts," Sarah replied, though she wasn't entirely sure anymore.
